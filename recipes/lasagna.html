Mom's Lasagna

Description

This recipe for lasagna is a family favorite. With a mix of hearty meatsauce and creamy bechamel, this is a recipe that even the most fickle eaters will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 package no boil lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb (16oz) mild italian sausage
  • 24 oz jar marinara sauce or preferred flavor of pasta sauce
  • 1/3 cup chicken stock
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 quart milk
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  • 3 cups mozzerella cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat until shimmering, add italian sausage and brown.
  3. When sausage is browned, add marinara sauce and chicken stock. Bring to simmer.
  4. Once the meat sauce mixture has been brought to a simmer, reduce heat to low.
  5. Heat a saucepan over medium heat, add butter and heat until foaming subsides.
  6. When foaming subsides, add all purpose flour and stir until slightly darkened with a nutty aroma.
  7. Add milk to the butter and flour mixture a bit at a time while stirring.
  8. Continue to stir until mixture comes to a rolling simmer then reduce heat.
  9. Once mixture has thickened, add salt and freshly grated nutmeg. Set aside.
  10. In the bottom of a large 9 x 13 baking dish spread a small amount of the meat mixture, then add a layer of noodles.
  11. Add a layer of meatsauce followed by a layer of the bechamel sauce. Cover with noodles and repeat process until all noodles, meat sauce and bechamel have been used.
  12. Cover the top of the lasagna with the mozzerella cheese then bake at 350 covered for 25 minutes.
  13. Once 25 minutes have elapsed, uncover the lasagna and raise heat to 450 degrees and bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes or until top of lasagna is golden brown and bubbly.

Turkey Tacos

Description

These tacos are a lighter take on an easy family favorite. By substituting ground turkey in lieu of ground beef, these tacos are healthier, lighter, and just as satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp canola oil
  • medium onion, diced finely
  • 16 oz 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 packet preferred taco seasoning (I use McCormick mild seasoning)
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • package of taco shells of choice
  • toppings of choice

Directions

  1. heat canola oil in a 12" nonstick pan over medium high heat.
  2. when oil is shimmering, add onion and saute until softened.
  3. add ground turkey and brown completely.
  4. when turkey is browned, add seasoning packet along with a small amount of water and soy sauce.
  5. bring mixture to a simmer then reduce heat to low and cook for 5 minutes
  6. add mixture to taco shells then top with all of your favorite taco-night toppings!
